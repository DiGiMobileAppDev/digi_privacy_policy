1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for our services
  • Express interest in our products
  • Participate in activities on our platform
  • Contact us for support

Information We Collect Includes:

  • Personal Details: Names, phone numbers, mailing addresses
  • Authentication Data: Passwords and account credentials
  • Location Data: Geolocation information for delivery services
  • Device Information: Mobile device details, operating system, browser type
  • Payment Information: Processed through our internal wallet system

Payment Processing: DIGI Express uses an internal wallet system. Users initiate payments externally by scanning QR codes through their own banking apps. We do not directly collect full credit card details.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create and maintain user accounts
  • Service Delivery: Facilitate international delivery services
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
  • Order Management: Process purchases, payments, and deliveries
  • Security: Protect our services and prevent fraud
  • Analytics: Improve our services and user experience

3. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your information based on:

  • Consent: When you give us permission for specific purposes
  • Contract Performance: To fulfill our services to you
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and ensure security
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help deliver our services
  • Google Services: Google Maps SDK for location services
  • Business Partners: Only when necessary for order fulfillment

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain security and prevent fraud
  • Remember your preferences

  • Provide personalized experiences

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. When you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where retention is required for legal compliance.

7. Information Security

8. Information from Minors
